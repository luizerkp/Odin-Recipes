As the title says this is a very simple sous vide steak recepie. Sous Vide is a method of cooking vacuum sealed food, specially meats and fish, in a warm water bath for long period of time anywhere from 1 to 36hrs depending of the food being cooked. This method makes cooking a tender steak to the desired temperature much easier and consistant. Its main downsisde is the amount of time it takes to cook the steak and the extra equipment requirement. You will need a sous vide capable machine, there are many out there and prices can range from as low as $59.99 to $500+. I recomend the Anova precision or the Ninja foodi if you are looking an air-fryer combo. Last but not least to cook the perfect sous vide steak you will also need a grill, cast iron pan, air-fryer or oven.
